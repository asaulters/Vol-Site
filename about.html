Who We Are

VolSite(think of name) was created to benefit both organizations and developers. We know that starting anything is hard, so we believe that everyone could use some help.

We have been in both situations. We have created organizations and nonprofits, scrounging up enough money to buy a $100 website off Fiverr and cold-called other businesses asking to work on their sites for free to try to get legitimate real-world client experience.

What has this taught us?

Neither of those are very exciting. They add just a little more difficulty to an already tough situation.

Here, we don't believe you need any more on your plate. We believe that there is a way both sides of that coin can get what they want without all the extra. This is why VolSite (new name needed still) was created. We are here to connect new organizations to new developers willing to work together on projects to help each other.

For Organizations

We know there is a lot on your plate already. Let us take a little of that burden away. Here we give you the ability to have developers contact you about your perfect website and create it for FREE. We know you've better things to do and think about than this. Let us take a little of that burden away.

For Developers

Let's be real, finding that first job is not the easiest thing to do. We've all heard it, you need experience to get a job, yet you need that job to get experience. Here you can search through job opportunities to find ones you are excited to help with. Grow your portfolio and experience with us.
